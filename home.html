Welcome to Sydney High School

The Mission of Sydney High School is to prepare students for the future by striving for academic excellence, and seeing that they grow into responsible citizens who are engaged in a global community.
Sydney High School proudly works closely together with industry leader companies, provides the latest tools for our students and gets involved in community events.

  • Great teachers
  • Fun environment
